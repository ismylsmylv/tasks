Homemade Apple Pie Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 package of pre-made pie crusts
  • 6-8 medium-sized apples
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons butter

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425Â°F
  2. Roll out one of the pie crusts and place it in a 9-inch pie dish.
  3. Peel, core, and thinly slice the apples.
  4. In a large bowl, mix the apple slices with sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg, flour, and lemon juice until well combined.
  5. Pour the apple mixture into the pie crust in the pie dish. Spread it out evenly.
  6. Dot the top of the apples with small pieces of butter.
  7. Roll out the second pie crust and place it over the apples. Trim any excess dough and crimp the edges to seal the pie.
  8. If desired, brush the top crust with the egg wash for a shiny finish.
  9. Cut a few small slits in the top crust to allow steam to escape.
  10. Place the pie in the preheated oven and bake for 45-55 minutes or until the crust is golden brown and the filling is bubbling.
  11. Remove the pie from the oven and let it cool for at least 30 minutes before serving.

Serving suggestion:

You can serve it with a scoop of vanilla ice cream for extra deliciousness.

Enjoy!
